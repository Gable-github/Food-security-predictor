Welcome to food price predictor. Here, you can view our price prediction models for various events. For example, you can predict the price of food due to a change in climate patterns, government agricultural technology funding, etc.

Here's a quick guide!

  • Click on the menu above to navigate to different price predictors for different variables.
  • In one page, you will find a visuallization of our linear/multiple regression model for that factor and relevant details (e.g. 5 point summary).
  • You can then input a specific value for that variable to see the specific price at that variable.
